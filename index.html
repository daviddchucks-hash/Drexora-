About Me

Full-Stack Developer

Hi, I'm David Chucks

I'm a web developer and the founder of Drexora, a premium web development brand dedicated to creating stunning digital experiences. Based in Nigeria, I help businesses, churches, and individuals establish powerful online presences that drive real results.

With over 3 years of hands-on experience in front-end and full-stack development, I specialize in crafting responsive, performant websites using modern technologies like HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, and Firebase. I take pride in writing clean, maintainable code that's built to last.

My mission is simple: deliver premium quality at affordable prices. Every project I take on receives my full attention and commitment — from the first pixel to the final deployment on GitHub Pages.
